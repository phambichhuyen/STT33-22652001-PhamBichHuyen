Welcome

At Diamond Health Club, you can stay healthy year-round and have fun doing it! We offer something for everyone. Our state-of-the-art facilities can challenge the most seasoned athlet first-time users. Be sure to check out our great classes for everyone from children and teens to adults and seniors.
